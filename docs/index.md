# Nertzy Support

Nertzy is the classic NERTS card game — solitaire meets a footrace — for
iPhone and iPad.

## How to play

Everyone plays at once; there are no turns. Race your opponents to play
cards onto the shared piles in the middle of the table (aces start piles,
build up by suit), dig through your own Nerts pile, and hit the big red
button when it's empty. **+1 point** for every card you play to the table,
**−2** for every card left in your Nerts pile. First to 100 wins.

- **Tap** your deck to flip three cards; **tap** any exposed card to send
  it to the table
- **Drag** cards between your work piles (build down, alternating colors)
- **Undo** takes back your last move — the button above your discard pile
- Stuck? Open the pause menu and use **Table Shuffle**

## Common questions

**The sound stopped working.**
Check the silent switch on the side of your device — Nertzy respects it.
Sound also pauses politely for phone calls and Siri, and comes back on its
own afterward.

**The game paused by itself.**
Nertzy pauses whenever you leave the app (calls, app switching, locking
your phone) so the robots can't play while you're not looking. Tap Resume
to continue.

**Where did the finished piles go?**
When a pile reaches its king, the king flips face down and the pile leaves
the table to keep things tidy. Those cards still count in the scoring.
