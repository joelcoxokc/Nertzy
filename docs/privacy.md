# Nertzy Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 7, 2026**

Nertzy is a card game. It was built to be played, not to collect data.

## What we collect

Nothing.

Nertzy does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal
information or usage data. Specifically, the app has:

- **No accounts** — there is nothing to sign up for
- **No analytics** — we don't track how you play
- **No advertising** — and no ad networks or tracking SDKs
- **No third-party services** — the app makes no network connections at all

## What stays on your device

Your game preferences (number of opponents and table speed) are stored
locally on your device using Apple's standard app storage. They never leave
your phone, and deleting the app deletes them.

## Children's privacy

Because Nertzy collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from
children.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of Nertzy ever changes any of the above, this policy
will be updated before that version ships, and the change will be noted in
the App Store release notes.
